About RevSec.io

Exploring cybersecurity through practice, precision, and persistence.

The Project

Welcome to RevSec.io, a personal cybersecurity lab and blog focused on blue-team defense, incident response, and digital forensics.

The Vision

My vision is to simply have a place to put all my ideas, interests and experiments, RevSec.io is an outlet for this; the best way for me to get everything out of my head and out somewhere where it might help someone else. Even when I make mistakes, they’re worth learning and documenting. My long term goal is to keep everything on this site free and available for everyone; just like the tools and resources that got me started. This means no pop up ads or paywalls behind any article, I will never restrict any content I make because I personally can’t stand it.
