About RevSec.io

Exploring cybersecurity through practice, precision, and persistence.

The Project

Welcome to RevSec.io, a personal cybersecurity blog focused on blue team defence and digital forensics. Included are hands-on projects, links to open-source tools, and other resources. I’m a complete beginner figuring things out as I go and this site is where I document it all. Hopefully, you’ll learn something along the way too.

If you notice any mistakes or want to drop some feedback, I’d really appreciate it; contact me at feedback@revsec.io.

The Vision

My vision is to simply have a place to put all my ideas, interests, and experiments. RevSec.io is an outlet for this, the best way for me to get everything out of my head and out somewhere where it might help someone else. Even when I make mistakes, they’re worth learning and documenting.

My long-term goal is to keep everything on this site free and available for everyone, just like the tools and resources that got me started. This means no pop-up ads or paywalls behind any article. I will never restrict any content I make because I personally can’t stand it.
